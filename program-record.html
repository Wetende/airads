Course description

Artificial intelligence increasingly impacts how we study, work, and communicate. You likely already interact with AI via navigation apps, chatbots, or search engines. This practical course uses simple language and real-life examples to explain AI concepts without requiring a technical background. By the end, you will confidently explain how AI works, use AI tools responsibly, and critically evaluate AI-generated results.
